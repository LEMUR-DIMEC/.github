# LEMUR https://laboratoriolemur.cl/

LEMUR is a research and development laboratory at the Mechanical Engineering Department at Universidad de Chile. It focuses on innovation in engineering fields such as mechatronics, robotics, mechanical design, and computer vision.



## Main Projects

### Perrito - Quadruped robot

This project was born in early 2024 at the [Mechanical Engineering Department](https://dimec.uchile.cl/) by Design of Mechanical Systems students.
